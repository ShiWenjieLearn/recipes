Ingredients:
	-	3 oranges
	-	1 knife
	-	1 big plate

Directions:
1. Wash the oranges and put them on a platsurface of some steady platform.
2. Cut the oranges into round pieces.
3. Get rid of the peel of the round pieces by taking straight cuts along the edge.
4. Place the pieces in a beautiful way in the plate.
